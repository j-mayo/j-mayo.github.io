About Me

I am an M.S. student at KAIST, Kim Jaechul Graduate School of AI, and a member of the CVML Lab, My current research focuses on Generative Modeling, 3D Geometry and Vision-Language Action Models.

I am driven by a desire to leverage artificial intelligence to solve complex real-world challenges and push the boundaries of multimodal understanding. I believe in developing robust, scalable AI systems that seamlessly connect perception with action.

Beyond academics, I enjoy playing the piano.
